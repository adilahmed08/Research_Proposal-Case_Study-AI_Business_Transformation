# AI-Powered Business Transformation

This repository contains a short research case study titled:

**“AI-Powered Business Transformation: Case Studies of Amazon and eBag in Shaping Innovative Business Models.”**

## Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming modern businesses by improving decision-making, optimizing operations, and enhancing customer experiences. This paper explores how AI technologies are used in the online retail sector to create efficient and innovative business models.

The study focuses on two companies:

- **Amazon** – A global e-commerce leader using AI for recommendation systems, logistics optimization, and inventory management.
- **eBag** – A Bulgarian online supermarket applying AI to automate processes, predict consumer demand, and optimize inventory.

By comparing these two companies, the paper highlights how AI helps businesses:

- Improve operational efficiency
- Enhance customer experiences
- Optimize supply chain and inventory management
- Support data-driven decision making

## Key Topics Covered

- Artificial Intelligence in business
- Machine Learning and predictive analytics
- AI-powered recommendation systems
- Inventory and supply chain optimization
- AI-driven customer personalization
- Business transformation in the online retail sector

## Contents

- `AI-Research Case Study.pdf` – The research paper discussing AI-driven business transformation using Amazon and eBag as case studies.

## Purpose

The goal of this study is to provide insight into how companies leverage AI technologies to innovate their business models and remain competitive in the digital marketplace.
